RECIPE

Halwa (Indian Sweet) by Nithya Kondagari

Ingredients:

4 tbsp Ghee

1 cup semolina

1 cup sugar

2 cups water

Cashews for garnish (optional)

Directions: Melt the ghee on a pan on medium heat and fry the cashews lightly and keep them aside. In the same pan, add semolina and fry it for 5 minutes In another pan, heat the pan to just before boiling, and add it to the semolina. Mix for a few minutes, then add sugar and mix again until it is completely mixed together. Transfer to a bowl and put the fried cashews from earlier on it. Enjoy!
