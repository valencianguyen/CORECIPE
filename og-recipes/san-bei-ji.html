RECIPE

三杯鸡 San Bei Ji (Three Cup Chicken) by Rebecca Austin

Ingredients:

6 chicken thighs (blanched and cut into chunks)

2 tablespoons seasame oil

15 slices of ginger

10 cloves of garlic

½ cup cooking rice wine

2 tablespoons light soy sauce

1 tablespoon dark soy sauce

1 tablespoon rock sugar

as much basil as you want

Directions: Heat up the sesame oil with the ginger and garlic in a pot. Put in the chicken, then rice wine, both soy sauces, and then the sugar. Let everything simmer with the lid on. Right before serving, put in the basil and cook it down a little bit (like 5 min depending on heat). Serve over rice! Enjoy!
