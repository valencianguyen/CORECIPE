RECIPE

Momo by Ansuya

Ingredients:

For the filling:

Ground meat of choice

Vegtables of choice

Spices

Salt

Oil

For the dough:

Flour

Salt

Oil

Water

For the Momo ko aachar:

Tomato

Green chili peppers

Tumeric

Corriander

Directions: So first you mix the ground meat and (grained) vegetables together. Then you mix salt/other spices and oil with the filling mix. Boom, Filling done. Then you wrap little circular handfuls of filling mix with the dumpling wraps. Then you steam the dumplings till they’re cooked. While they’re cooking you can make the tomato sauce by blending all the mentioned ingredients together. Then once your dumplings are cooked, you eat them with the side of tomato sauce/pickle. Enjoy!
