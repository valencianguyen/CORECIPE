RECIPE

Sour Cream Pancakes by Rebecca Austin

Ingredients:

1 ½ cups flour

3 tablespoons raw brown sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt

½ cup sour cream

¾ cup and 1 tablespoon milk

2 extra-large eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 ½ tablespoons unsalted butter

Directions: Mix all the ingredients together, but don't overmix! Then make the pancakes on the stove with medium-heat. Enjoy!
